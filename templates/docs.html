Documentation

Learn how to use Land Measurement effectively.

Getting Started

Welcome! Here's how to get started with the platform:

  • Guest Mode: You can try our calculation tools immediately without an account. Note that your work will not be saved.
  • Register Account: Create a free account to save all your projects, access them from any device, and manage your data in the cloud.
  • Login: Access your personal dashboard with all your saved projects.

How to Use Leveling Calculations

Our Sipat Datar (Leveling) tool is designed for speed and accuracy.

  1. Navigate to the "Leveling" tool from your dashboard or the guest mode portal.
  2. Input Data: You can either:
    • Upload an Excel file (template provided).
    • Manually enter your station points, BS (Backsight), and FS (Foresight) data into the table.
  3. (Optional) 3-Wire Validation: If you used 3-wire leveling, enter your Top, Mid, and Bot readings. The system will automatically validate the accuracy (Top + Bot) / 2 = Mid and flag any errors.
  4. Process: Click the "Calculate" button.
  5. View Results: The system will instantly generate a complete table with Rise/Fall, Elevation (H), and corrections. You will also see an interactive Plotly graph visualizing the ground profile.

How to Use Cut & Fill Calculations

Calculate excavation and embankment volumes with ease.

  1. Select the "Cut & Fill" tool.
  2. Input Data: Provide your stationing (STA) and existing ground elevation data.
  3. Set Design Elevation: Enter your proposed design elevation (this can be a single level or a varying slope).
  4. Process: Click "Calculate Volume".
  5. View Results: You will receive a detailed report showing the Cut (Galian) and Fill (Timbunan) volume at each station, as well as the total net volume.

Understanding Your Results

Your data is more than just numbers.

  • Interactive Graphs: All calculation results are paired with Plotly graphs. You can zoom, pan, and hover over data points to get detailed information.
  • Gemini AI Reports: For logged-in users, you can click "Generate AI Analysis". Gemini will review your results and provide insights, such as identifying potential reading errors, noting the steepest slopes, or summarizing the overall project.
  • Excel Export: Click the "Export to Excel" button at any time to download a formatted `.xlsx` file containing both your raw input data and the complete calculated results.
